Henri's Surf Journal

I decided to write a short report of each of my sessions. The goals are (i) to bore my non-surfing friends with surf talk; (ii) to entertain my surfing friend with surf talk; but really, to read this in 30 years and to be brought back to all these sessions.
I learned how to surf in the San Diego area and surfed there 2001-2005. On August 2005 I moved to the island of O`ahu in Hawai`i.
