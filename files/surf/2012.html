Mon 4 Jun 2012

  • Flies, 8:15AM-9:30AM
  • On my 9'6''.
  • By myself. Up to 6 people out, including Eric and Jim.
  • Waist- to chest-high waves, with a few head-high peaks, nice and consistent at first and degrading as the wind picked up.
  • Caught about 8 waves, with a few fun ones, but in particular and absolutely outstanding right. I took off a bit deep and thought that as usual the second steep section would close out in front of me. Surprisingly, it stayed barely open and taking a super high line I was able to make it through although it was getting quite vertical. Some guy on the 3rd section was ready to drop in because of the typical close-out, but pulled back as I zipped through that 3rd section again on a very high-line, eventually cutting back a bit to weave my way to the inside. Definitely the best wave I have surfed in at least a year. The one after was even better, and that guy whom I've seen before a few times had a fantastic ride. While my cutback were rather smooth and non-radical, his were quite brutal on his 9ft board. Only two waves on that set, and definitely the waves of the day.
